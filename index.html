About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Anhui University, supervised by Prof. Bo Jiang (AHU), jointly supervised by Prof. Jian Liang (CASIA) and Prof. Aihua Zheng (AHU).
From 2020 to 2022, I am a visiting student in Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, supervised by Prof. Ran He. Before that, I received bachelor's degree in Anhui University in 2019.
My research interests focus on transfer learning and their applications.
